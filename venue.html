The 2020 CSSS meeting will be held at the University of Prince Edward Island. Details on meetings rooms and parking will be updated when the program is finalized. 550 University Ave, Charlottetown Prince Edward Island, Canada C1A 4P3

Accomodations
Andrew Hall

UPEI’s suite style accommodations are available in both 1 and 2 bedroom configurations. Each suite features 1 or 2 bedrooms with double bed in each bedroom, private washroom, microwave, mini-fridge, Wifi, cable TV and AC. Nightly rate: $108.00 (+ HST).

Blanchard Hall

UPEI’s apartment style residence. Each apartment has 2 bedrooms with 2 single beds in each (4 total). The apartment also features a full kitchen, living room, wifi and cable TV. Nightly rate: $108 (+ HST).

Bernardine Hall

UPEI’s traditional residence provides clean affordable nightly accommodations. Rooms are available in double or single configuration. Rooms feature single beds, wifi, and share a washroom with the next room beside. Nightly rates: Single $47.00 Double $62.00 (+ HST).


Delegates can make their reservations by calling (902)-566-0442 or by emailing conference@upei.ca. Please make sure to mention Canadian Soil Science Meeting, booking code #17550 to get the preferred nightly rates.
