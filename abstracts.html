Abstract Submission Presentation Guidelines





CSSS 2021 Virtual Meeting June 7-10


Abstract submission is now closed and presenters have been notified.

Please contact us if you submitted an abstract but did not receive an email - csss2021.annualmeeting@gmail.com.



Guidelines for poster and oral presentations

• All presenters have been emailed a link to upload their presentations. Presenters must register in order to access the conference platform. If you did not receive an email please contact us at csss2021.annualmeeting@gmail.com.

• The conference will be delivered over a professional platform, Pheedloop, and will be managed by a highly competent and very experienced technical team. The conference platform is rich with networking features such as text-based chat, video based chat, and group chat. The duration of the pre-recorded presentations must be strictly respected.



Poster presentations

• All poster presentations have to be pre-recorded video presentations and must be no longer than 5 minutes. Slides-type presentation. It is not required that you prepare a traditional poster.
• Poster presentations will be available on demand with a designated question period in livestream for each session.


Oral presentations

• All oral presentations have to be pre-recorded, (except keynotes).
• The time allocated to each contributed talk is 15 minutes (12 minutes pre-recorded presentation + 3 minutes for questions), unless otherwise noted. Slides-type presentation.
• The question period will take place in livestream immediately following each pre-recorded presentation.


Important details

• The deadline to upload your presentation is May 25, 2021. All presenters should have received the link by email.
• You are free to choose the style of presentation you want, you can decide to record only your slides, yourself or both.
• Presentations must be submitted in .mp4 format and no larger than 1GB. Zoom saves pre-recorded presentations using .mp4 format and PowerPoint has the option to save as .mp4.
• The following formats are NOT accepted: .wmv, .mov, .ppsx, .pptx, .ppt, .pps.
• Use a minimum of font size 24 when preparing your power point. Your presentation format should be 16 x 9, and resolution 1080p x 1920p.

If you are no longer able to participate in the virtual meeting, please inform us asap at csss2021.annualmeeting@gmail.com.
