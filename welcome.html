CSSS / SCSS 2020 Anual Meeting

June 21st - 25th, Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island. Meeeting to be held at the University of Prince Edward Island

Greetings fellow soil enthusiasts!

We are excited to be hosting the 2019 joint meeting of the Canadian Society of Soil Science and Canadian Society for Agricultural and Forest Meteorology here in Saskatoon. This is a special year for us as it marks the 100th anniversary of the Department of Soil Science at the University of Saskatchewan, hence the theme: “Profiling Change – A century of soil science”. The conference will kick off with a symposium on Shining the Light on Soils, jointly held with Rhizosphere5 (rhizo5.org), an international conference happening concurrently here in Saskatoon, and an anniversary reception downtown. Following that, we are planning two full days of sessions exploring the latest advances in all aspects of our discipline, plus special symposia on remediation/reclamation and education in soil science, the latter being a tribute to our late colleague, Professor Terry Tollefson.

The conference will be followed by a full slate of field trips, including a pedology-focused exploration of our unique Prairie soils, a visit to local restoration sites, and an agronomy tour down to the Schoenau farm in south-central Saskatchewan. We also have great opportunities for students to network through our pre-conference Soil Judging Contest and a social event on the Thursday evening. For the greenhouse gas aficionados in the crowd, stick around for a workshop on measurement techniques hosted by Rich Farrell.

Overall, it promises to be a great week of soil science and a fabulous opportunity to celebrate a century of Soil Science!

We look forward to welcoming you in 2019
