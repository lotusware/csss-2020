WELCOME TO CHARLOTTETOWN, PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND

Dear 2020 CSSS participants,

We are excited to host the 2020 annual meeting of the Canadian Society of Soil Science in Charlottetown from June 21 to 25th, 2020. The organizing committee welcomes you at the birthplace of confederation where agriculture, tourism and fishing represent the largest industries. The meeting will start with interesting pre-conference workshops (details to follow) planned on Sunday the 21st, followed by three full days of sessions and the fourth day will be dedicated to field trips (details to follow). The theme of the meeting is “Building bridges and connecting soil processes”. The keynote speakers and scientific sessions will cover diverse and interesting topics including: soil ecology, impact of agricultural practices on soil health, linking soil organisms to ecosystems services, soil nutrient management and water quality, greenhouse gas measurements and modelling, precision agriculture and big data management, along with methods of soil mapping.

We look forward to seeing you in Charlottetown. The island will charm you with its beautiful beaches, red sands and cliffs, and more importantly with its cuisine characterized by fresh and various local ingredients. The meeting will correspond with the beginning of lobster season.
