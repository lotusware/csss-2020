WELCOME TO CHARLOTTETOWN, PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND

Dear 2020 CSSS participants, We are excited to host the 2020 annual meeting of the Canadian Society of Soil Science in Charlottetown from June 21 to 25th, 2020 under the theme of “Building bridges and connecting soil processes”. The organizing committee welcomes you at the birthplace of confederation where agriculture, tourism and fishing represent the largest industries.

The meeting will start with interesting pre-conference workshops (details to follow) planned on Sunday the 21st followed by three full days of sessions and the fourth day will be dedicated to field trips (details to follow). The island will charm you with its beautiful beaches, red sands and cliffs, and more importantly with its cuisine characterized by fresh and various local ingredients. The meeting will correspond with the beginning of lobster season.

We encourage you to come early or to stay late. We look forward to seeing you in Charlottetown.
